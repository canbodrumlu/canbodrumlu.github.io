I was a theatre kid (to be honest, I had more experience in puppeteering). Nowadays, theatre is my way out of all the chaos of academia. I realized I have no record of the plays I have been seeing since I moved to Seattle except the playbills collecting dust in my apartment. That's why I started keeping a running log of the plays I've gone to see, where I saw them, and maybe a quick note on what I thought of it.
